Our Mission

Making your connection with nature attainable.

More than a Mission, a Purpose.

Our mission at With the Land is to help everyone from beginners to experienced outdoors people build and make your connection to nature attainable. We hope to help people connect with the land by providing educational content through different platforms so that everyone can find what is right for them.

As the creator of With the Land, I believe that as humans we must reconnect and rebuild our relationships with the natural environments around us to ensure they are protected and preserved for all life to use safely and sustainably for generations to come.

Luke Seabrook, With The Land

With the Land will provide educational content presented in enjoyable ways for beginners to experienced outdoors people to use, so there is something for everyone. Our goal is to provide you with the knowledge you need to build and or strengthen your connection with nature. The natural world is an amazing, alluring, and beautiful place for all to enjoy and we hope to help you experience this beautiful planet for yourself, safely, responsibly and sustainably.
